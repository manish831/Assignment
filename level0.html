What is treasure Hunt?

A treasure hunt is a game or activity where participants are given clues or a map to follow in order to find a hidden treasure or prize. The treasure can be anything from a small token or trinket to a large sum of money or valuable object. Treasure hunts can be organized for a variety of reasons, including as a fun activity for children's parties, corporate team building exercises, or community events. The goal of the game is to encourage problem-solving, teamwork, and creativity as participants work together to decipher clues and uncover the hidden treasure.
